# Vendor Interview Notes — Day 1

Vendors talked to: 2–5

## Q1 — How do you currently track what you sell and spend each day?

- Vendors split into two groups:
  - Group A: write it down (notebook or phone notes)
  - Group B: keep it in memory, no written record

Takeaway: Two types of users to design for — one already tracks manually, one tracks nothing at all.

## Q2 — If you could just talk into your phone to log a sale, would that help?

- All vendors asked said YES, it would be helpful.

Takeaway: Strong validation — the idea is confirmed useful.

## Q3 — What phrases would they naturally say?

- Captured: vendors greet customers with "what do you want / what do you need?" when someone approaches to buy — this is the vendor's opening line to a customer, not their own sale report.
- Vendor-side sale phrasing CONFIRMED. Pattern: [item] + [quantity] + [price] + ሸጥኩኝ (I sold)
  Example: "ሽንኩርት ሁለት ኪሎ በመቶ ብር ሸጥኩኝ" (I sold 2 kilos of onions for 100 birr)

Q3 GAP: CLOSED ✅

## Summary

- Validation: strong, 100% positive response to the voice-logging idea
- Sale phrasing pattern confirmed: item + quantity + price + ሸጥኩኝ
